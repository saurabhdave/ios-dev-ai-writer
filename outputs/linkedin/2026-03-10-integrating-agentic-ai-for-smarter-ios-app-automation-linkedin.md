🚀 As iOS apps become more complex, traditional automation scripts just don’t cut it anymore. Static test flows break with UI changes, monitoring feels reactive, and manual upkeep grows costly. The future lies in integrating agentic AI—autonomous agents that adapt, learn, and make decisions in real time—to transform how we automate testing, monitoring, and workflows on iOS.

From my experience designing these systems, here are practical insights to help you get started:

• Focus agentic AI on tasks that are repetitive but dynamic, requiring contextual awareness and adaptation.  
• Structure agents with clear goals, state awareness, and feedback loops to continuously improve their decisions.  
• Leverage Swift Concurrency and Combine to keep agents responsive without blocking the main thread.  
• Balance on-device inference for privacy and latency with cloud-based model updates for scalability.  
• Always implement robust monitoring, manual overrides, and fallback plans to maintain control and trust.

Agentic AI isn’t a silver bullet—it adds complexity and requires careful design—but when done right, it future-proofs your automation against evolving app landscapes.
